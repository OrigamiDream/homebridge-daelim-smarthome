# CHANGELOGS

- Add support for Smart eLife app.
  - Air conditioners with cooling, dehumiditifying and air purifying.
  - HomeKit Secure Video for showing a visitor's snapshot on the doorbell.
  - Motion sensors for the front door and car entrance.
  - Calling elevator with arrival notification.
  - Locking gas valve remotely (irreversible).
  - Thermostats for precise room temperature adjustment.
  - Indoor air quality sensors for PM-10, PM-2.5 and VOCs density.
  - Lightbulbs with brightness and color temperature adjustment.
  - Outlets support.
  - Vent for air purifying with fan rotation speed adjustment.
- Revamped configuration UI with provider (daelim, smart-elife) selectors.
